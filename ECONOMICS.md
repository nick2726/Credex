# ZenAudit Unit Economics

## 1. Converted Lead Value
A converted lead for Credex (switching to discounted credits) is worth an estimated **$1,500 LTV** (Lifetime Value).
- **Logic:** Average startup AI spend is ~$2k/mo. Credex takes a ~10-15% margin on credits. Retention ~12 months before growth/exit.

## 2. Target CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)
- **Direct Lead Gen:** Aiming for <$50 CAC.
- Since ZenAudit is a free tool, the "cost" is primarily development and hosting. Viral loops (Shareable URLs) bring CAC down towards $0.

## 3. Conversion Funnel Math
- **Audit Completed:** 100%
- **Captured Lead (Email):** 20%
- **Credex Consultation Booked:** 5% (Targeted at >$500/mo savings leads)
- **Credit Purchase:** 1% of total audits
- **Profitability:** If 1 audit out of 100 buys credits ($1,500), we can afford to spend $15 per audit.

## 4. Path to $1M ARR in 18 Months
To drive $1M ARR, we need ~$83k monthly revenue.
- At $200 margin/customer/month, we need **415 active customers**.
- At a 1% "Audit to Customer" conversion, we need **41,500 total audits**.
- Over 18 months, that is ~2,300 audits/month or ~75 audits/day.
- This is highly achievable with the GTM plan stated.

## 5. Math Breakdown
- **Daily Audits:** 75
- **Monthly Audits:** 2,250
- **Leads (50% email capture):** 1,125
- **Close rate (1%):** 11.25 new customers/month.
- **CAC Analysis:** If virality is 0.2 (every 5 users bring 1 new), the organic growth engine makes this a top-tier lead-gen asset.
